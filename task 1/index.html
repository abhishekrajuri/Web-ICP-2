About Trees

Trees are the one of the very useful and beautiful living things on the Earth.
They provide us fresh oxygen and many necessary things, like wood, pulp for
producing the paper, fruits, shade in the sunny days, and provide the home
to a lot of animals including land animals, insects, and birds.

Types of trees

  1. Evergreen Trees
  2. Deciduous Trees

The parts of a tree

  • Roots
  • Trunk
  • Branches
  • Leaves

Trees use carbon dioxide to produce food.
A single tree can consume about 21 kg's of carbon dioxide each year.
